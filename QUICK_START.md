## 🎮 How to Use Each Section

### SECTION 0: Command Bar
**What it does:** Shows overall market health and your active filters

**How to use:**
1. **Search Bar**: Type stock code (e.g., BBCA)
2. **Regime Badge**: Shows if market is BULLISH/BEARISH + volatility level
3. **Ticker**: Watch global commodities and indices for correlation hints
4. **Health LEDs**: 
   - 🟢 Green = All good
   - 🔴 Red = Problem (check logs)

**💡 Trading Tip:** If market is UPTREND + VOL:LOW → Best for long entries. If DOWNTREND + VOL:HIGH → Avoid large positions.

---

### SECTION 1: Market Intelligence Canvas
**What it does:** Real-time buy/sell pressure analysis

**Key Metrics:**
- **UPS Score (0-100)**
  - 90+ 🟢 STRONG_BUY: Aggressive accumulation
  - 70-90 🟢 BUY: Good buying pressure
  - 50-70 🟡 NEUTRAL: Balanced
  - 30-50 🟠 SELL: Selling pressure
  - <30 🔴 STRONG_SELL: Distribution in progress

- **HAKA vs HAKI %**
  - HAKA = Aggressive BUY (price ≥ ask/offer)
  - HAKI = Aggressive SELL (price ≤ bid)
  - If HAKA > 60%: Strong buying momentum
  - If HAKI > 50%: Be careful, sellers are winning

- **Pressure Index**
  - Positive (+) = More buying than selling
  - Negative (-) = More selling than buying
  - ±10% or more = Strong signal

**💡 Trading Tip:** Use UPS 75+ for entry. Combine with broker flow (Section 2) for confirmation.

---

### SECTION 2: The Flow Engine
**What it does:** Identify major players (whales) and their trading patterns

**Controls:**
- **FILTER**
  - 🎭 MIX: All brokers
  - 🐋 WHALE: Only top institutional traders (Z > 2.5)
  - 👥 RETAIL: Only small retail traders (Z < -1)
  - 🧠 SMART MONEY: Consistent professionals

- **TIMELINE**
  - 1 Day: Intraday accumulation
  - 7 Days: Weekly trend
  - 14 Days: Bi-weekly pattern
  - 21 Days: Monthly outlook

**Heatmap Explanation:**
- 7 mini-bars = 7 days of data
- Green bars = Days with buying
- Red bars = Days with selling
- Brightness = Volume intensity

**Wash Sale Alert:**
- 🚨 Score > 60%: Suspicious pattern detected
- High volume but low net accumulation = traders manipulating
- **Action**: Wait for confirmation before entering

**💡 Trading Tip:** Find the whale with consistent buying (Z-Score > 2.5 for >5 days). Follow their entry point as your confirmation.

---

### SECTION 3: Neural Narrative Hub
**What it does:** AI-generated insights + automated stock screener

#### Part A: AI Narratives
Gemini AI analyzes:
- Current broker behavior
- Market regime conditions
- Provides 2-3 sentence summary
- Gives actionable recommendation

**How to read:**
- 🟢 Green background = Bullish signal
- 🔴 Red background = Bearish signal
- 🟡 Yellow = Neutral, wait for breakout

#### Part B: AI Screener
**Two modes:**

**1. 🚀 Daytrade Mode**
- Looks for: High volatility + HAKA dominance
- Holds: 1-4 hours
- Signal > 75: Strong scalping opportunity
- Use: For quick profits on momentum

**2. 📋 Swing Mode**
- Looks for: Whale accumulation + solid technicals
- Holds: 2-5 days
- Signal >65: Good swing trade potential
- Use: Hold positions longer for bigger profits

**Using the screener:**
1. Set price range (e.g., 100-500 IDR = penny stocks)
2. Click mode (Daytrade vs Swing)
3. Top 10 results show best opportunities
4. Click [Details] for full analysis
5. Click [Alert] to set price notification

**💡 Trading Tip:** Combine screener results with Section 2 (whale flow). If screener says BUY and whales are accumulating = Double confirmation!

### SECTION 4: Risk & Tactical Dock
**What it does:** Position sizing + recent trade activity

**Smart Position Sizing:**

**Recent Trades:**

- 🔴 HAKI (down arrow) = Aggressive SELL
- ⚪ NORMAL = Regular trade

**💡 Trading Tip:** If you see many HAKAs but screener shows SELL signal = Reversal coming soon. Be ready to short.

## ⚠️ Important Rules

### 1. **Only trade with confluences (multiple signals agree)**
- Section 1 (UPS) ✓
- Section 2 (Whale flow) ✓
- Section 3 (AI + Screener) ✓
- = 3+ confirmations = HIGH confidence

### 2. **Always use stop loss**
- Based on ATR (Section 4 recommends)
- Never risk more than 2% of capital per trade
- Set it BEFORE entering

### 3. **Don't over-trade**
- Screener shows 10 candidates, but
- Only trade top 3 with strongest signals
- Quality > Quantity

### 4. **Watch the health dashboard**
- If any LED goes red → data integrity issue
- Don't trade in doubt
- Wait for green lights

### 5. **Respect market regime**
- UPTREND → Go LONG only
- DOWNTREND → Go SHORT or SKIP
- SIDEWAYS → Tight ranges only
- VOLATILE → Avoid large positions

## 🔥 Advanced Tips

### Tip 1: Whale Following
Find a whale with Z-Score > 2.5 for 5+ days straight → they're serious about this stock. Follow their average buy price as your entry target.

### Tip 2: Wash Sale Detection
When Wash Sale Score > 70% but UPS still high → Fake buying. Volume might spike but won't sustain. Use this to predict reversals.

### Tip 3: Screener Anomalies
If Daytrade screener shows 8 stocks with 75+ scores in same morning → Market-wide buying spree = Higher reversal risk.

### Tip 4: Regime Transitions
When RSI transitions from <45 to >50 in same candle + Volatility spikes → Market reversal likely. Enter SHORT positions.

### Tip 5: Global Correlation
If Gold + Oil both rising + IHSG rising = Strong institutional buying. Follow the whales in Section 2.

## 💬 FAQ

**Q: What if whale buys but AI says SELL?**
A: Trust the whale for 1-2 days, but plan for reversal soon. Use smaller position size.

**Q: Should I follow screener or whale flow?**
A: Both! Screener finds candidates, whale flow confirms. Use together.

**Q: When is best time to trade?**
A: 9:30-11:30 AM (morning peak volume) and 14:00-16:00 (closing hour consolidation).

**Q: How much should I risk per trade?**
A: Never more than 2% of total capital. So if you have 10M, max loss per trade = 200k.

**Q: Can I trade during lunch (11:30-13:00)?**
A: Low volume + wide spreads. Avoid if possible.
